About this project

At Prynt, we love instant photography. But with the thousands of photos stored on our phones, there’s never been an easy way to print those photos, so they become lost in our camera roll or social feeds and rarely looked at again. So we‘re here to bring those memories back to our lives, in a physical way. It is our mission to make memories tangible again for a new era of moment-makers.

That's why we created Prynt Pocket, the first-ever device to transform your smartphone into an instant camera.

We were blown away by the initial response from users following the release of our first product, Prynt Case! We compiled feedback from thousands of enthusiastic backers and retail buyers to develop a list of product requests including features to add, remove, or enhance for future versions. We started from scratch and began working on our second product, Prynt Pocket, that would bring an even better instant camera experience to our users.

Team Prynt is a small team of 20 located in downtown San Francisco. Each of us from different backgrounds, we hold a shared purpose to empower the creativity in everyone. We’re excited to launch this new campaign and can’t wait to develop Prynt Pocket for Android with all of you.

In 2014, we successfully completed our first Kickstarter campaign earning $1.5M to launch Prynt Case for iOs and Android in the U.S., France, and United Kingdom.

Since then, we worked alongside our community to iterate and develop our second version product, Prynt Pocket for iOs, which launched May 2017. The goal was to make a device to give users the smallest instant camera experience on the market. We created Prynt Pocket for iPhone (available here), now we want to make it happen for Android users.

Today, we’re back on Kickstarter! We received an overwhelming number of messages from users asking for Prynt Pocket compatibility with Android phones following launch. To make sure we are building a product Android users will love, we want to shape our final product with you through this crowdfunding campaign. We wholeheartedly invite you to join us again, provide your feedback, and help us create a new generation of storytelling together.

We want everyone to enjoy Prynt Pocket for Android! International shipping is available for backers all over the world. We've done our best to select the lowest shipping rates possible. Additional duties/taxes may apply depending on your destination country.

Risks and challenges

Manufacturing a new product is the riskiest and most expensive phase of the journey, so the money we are raising goes directly towards covering our tooling and upfront manufacturing costs.

Since our first successful Kickstarter campaign, we’ve established solid relationships with the suppliers we’re using for the Prynt Pocket. We’re all one team with a goal of producing the best quality product.
